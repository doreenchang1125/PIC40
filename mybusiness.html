About Us

Hi everyone! Doreen Chang, president and founder of Doreen's® Coffee.

I am currently a Junior at the University of California, Los Angeles, scheduling to graduate in 2019. For the past year, I've been running this company while taking classes at UCLA.

Coffee has always been my favorite beverage. As a Mathematics major at UCLA, coffee has been one of the most essential items keeping me going from day to day. Living in Los Angeles for 3 years, I have studied at various cafes and explored many new coffee shops, all of which I have developed a decent understanding of. Because of my passion for coffee, I was inspired to start my own coffee brand, selling only the best tasting quality coffee beans. Whether or not you are a coffee addict like me, or just someone who sips occasionally, I'm sure you would enjoy my coffee.♥
